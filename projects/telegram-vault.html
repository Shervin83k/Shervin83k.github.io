Project Overview

Telegram Vault is a secure, end-to-end encrypted password manager that leverages Telegram as its platform. It allows users to store, retrieve, and manage passwords securely through Telegram's encrypted messaging platform.

The project was born from the need for a password manager that's accessible anywhere, doesn't rely on traditional cloud storage, and maintains security even if a device is lost or compromised.

Key Features

  • End-to-End Encryption

    AES-128 encryption ensures passwords are secure before leaving your device

  • Cross-Platform Sync

    Access your passwords from any device with Telegram installed

  • Local First Architecture

    Primary storage is local SQLite with encrypted Telegram backup

  • Zero-Knowledge Design

    Only you have access to your encryption keys and passwords

  • Battle-Tested

    27/28 comprehensive tests passing under extreme conditions

  • Emergency Access

    Access critical accounts from any device when compromised

Real-World Scenarios

Emergency Access: Imagine your devices are compromised. With Telegram Vault, access your bank, email, and critical accounts from any borrowed device via Telegram.
Device Transition: Moving to a new phone? Your encrypted vault travels with your Telegram account while decryption keys stay only with you.
Security Through Separation: Data is stored encrypted in Telegram; keys stay with you. Even if data is intercepted, it's useless without your key.
